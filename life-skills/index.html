Life Skills

Health Counseling

Our health education efforts include the following activities:

  • Group Counseling Sessions
    Here, we educate our youth on health issues and discourage them from engaging in risky behaviour, i.e., alcohol and drug abuse or unsafe sex.

  • Individual Counseling Sessions
    Here, we offer our youth one-on-one support with their personal problems.

  • Recreational Activities
    Our sports and arts offerings form an integral part of our health education as they both encourage and are encouraged by a healthy lifestyle.

Technology Education

We are grateful to partner with Labdoo to help us open a Computer Lab and provide technology education to MIYOSHA youth as soon as possible.

Labdoo supports sustainable access to education by collecting laptops, equipping them with education software and distributing them to children worldwide.

You can find our Labdoo project page here.

As Labdoo operates on an entirely voluntary and carbon-neutral basis, we are constantly looking for volunteers who could carry one or two laptops to Dar es Salaam when traveling to Tanzania from countries where Labdoo collects laptops, especially from Germany.

If you are planning such travels and could imagine to help us bring technology to our youths, please get in touch!
